Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aura before you start.

What is Aura?
Aura is your AI wellness companion. Through gentle face scans, conversations with Eva or Atlas, and a private dream journal, Aura helps you build daily self-awareness and reflect on your patterns.
How does the face scan work?
A 30-second scan captures visual signals about your hydration, energy, stress, and skin. Aura turns these into mindful observations — never medical advice.
Is my face data shared?
Images are sent to OpenAI for analysis only. OpenAI does not retain images for training. We never sell or share your face data with advertisers. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
What is the Inner Circle?
Inner Circle lets you connect with up to 5 people who matter most. Share wellness signals and stay gently present in each other's daily rhythm — no phone numbers or contact scraping required.
What is Coffee Reading?
A modern twist on Turkish coffee fortune-telling (Kahve Falı). Take a photo of your coffee cup and Eva reads the story with a warm, AI-touched interpretation.
How much does Aura cost?
$18.99/month or $189.90/year (saves 17%). All new users get a 7-day free trial.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Manage your subscription anytime in your iOS or Android settings. No questions asked.
Is Aura medical advice?
No. Aura is for informational, lifestyle, and reflection purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. For medical concerns, consult a qualified professional.
What languages does Aura support?
Aura supports 7 languages: English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese (Brazil), Spanish, and Turkish. The app auto-detects your device language.
Do I need an account?
No. Aura uses anonymous authentication. Just download, confirm you're 18+, and start. No email, password, or sign-up required.
Who are Eva and Atlas?
Your AI companions. Eva offers a warm, supportive tone. Atlas offers a more structured and focused tone. Both adapt to your patterns and grow with you over time.
