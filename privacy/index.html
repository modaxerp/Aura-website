Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Aura Bionics Inc. (“Aura Bionics”, “we”, “our”) is a technology company based in Ontario, Canada. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal information when you voluntarily provide it, such as your email address for waitlist registration, updates, or support requests. We also collect basic device and usage information for app functionality and analytics. Users create an account to access app features. Account information including email address is stored securely.

2. Use of Information

Information collected is used solely to provide access to the Aura Bionics service, communicate updates, and improve app performance. Any visual or audio input used by the application is processed only to enable app functionality and only with user consent.

3. Data Processing & Retention

Scan images may be stored securely in the user’s private account to enable the Photo Journey feature. Audio input is transcribed and not stored as audio. Audio transcription is processed via OpenAI Whisper API and is subject to OpenAI’s data policies. Aura Bionics does not sell personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide app functionality: Firebase (Google), Anthropic (Claude API), OpenAI (GPT-4o, Whisper), ElevenLabs, and fal.ai. These services process data solely to enable app features and are subject to their own privacy policies.

5. No Medical or Diagnostic Use

Aura Bionics is a wellness and lifestyle technology product intended for informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or health assessments.

6. Legal Compliance

Aura Bionics operates in accordance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Ontario privacy laws.

7. Your Rights

Users may request access to or deletion of their personal information by contacting us.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact: info@aurabionics.com
