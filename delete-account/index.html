Delete Your Account — Aura Bionics

1. Overview

This page explains how you can request deletion of your Aura Bionics account and your associated data.

2. How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open the Aura Bionics app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap 'Delete Account'
  4. Confirm your choice

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

3. How to Delete Your Account (Web / If You Uninstalled the App)

If you no longer have access to the app, email us at privacy@aurabionics.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line 'Delete My Account'. We will verify your identity and process deletion within 30 days.

4. What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, user ID)
  • All facial scan photos and biometric data
  • Health and fitness records
  • Chat history with Eve and Atlas AI
  • Inner Circle messages and connections
  • Voice recordings and transcripts
  • Device identifiers

5. What Data Is Retained

For legal, regulatory, and security reasons, the following may be retained in a limited and anonymized form:

  • Payment and transaction records (retained for 7 years for tax compliance)
  • Abuse and fraud prevention signals (retained in hashed form)
  • Aggregated, non-identifying analytics data

Retention period: 30 days grace period after deletion request, after which all identifiable data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems.
